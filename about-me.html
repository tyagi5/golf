My Golf Journey

I started playing golf when I moved to Irvine when I was in second grade. My dad was the one who had introduced me to golf. When I was in 3rd or 4th grade, I stopping playing golf for about a year, before I started playing golf again and going on tournaments. My first golf "game" wasn't really a game. It was just a bunch of Japanese people who had gathered a group and played at a local golf course for 9 holes. Some of the people who played were really good. The golf course was called Newport Beach Golf Course and I still go there from time to time.

At first I was really bad so they gave me a really big handicap of over 20. Out of pure luck, I got first place. After that, I practiced and practiced. I went on a parent-child US kids tournament with my dad and got second place. Then I went on my first actual golf game. It was at Newport Beach. To get to the Players Tour, I had to get 8+ or less at least three times. I got 8+ all three times and got two 3rd places and one second place.

After that, I went on my first Player's Tour game at David L. Baker Golf Course. I hit a 6+. I was in the division for 8 to 11 year olds. This division only played 9 holes, but from the 12-14 division, you had to play 18 holes so I stopped entering golf games for over a year. After a while, my dad convinced me to play and I played at Heartwell Golf Course. I did horribly, but I played another game the next month at David L. Baker, which I did horribly as well. I went on a total of 13 tournaments in 2023.

My dad made me go on golf games other than David L. Baker and Heartwell so I started playing at Chester Washington Golf Course too. On my final game of the year, I played really good and got 3rd place with a score of 7+.

In 2024, I started playing at Dad Miller Golf Course too. Although I haven't gotten a medal yet, I have improved my score significantly from last year, when I hit 18+'s on a regular basis.

My Clubs

I use a Callaway Mavrik Driver that I got from my dad. I also use a TSI1 Wood and a TSR1 Hybrid. For my irons, I use T300 irons. I have a 5 iron to a 9 iron, a pitching wedge, and a 48 degree club. I have 3 Cleveland Zipcore Wedges that are 52, 56, and 60 degrees. Finally, I have a TaylorMade Spider Putter.

My bag is a blue and yellow Titleist and I use yellow golf balls because they are the easiest to find.
